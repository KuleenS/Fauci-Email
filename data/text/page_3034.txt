Recent studies indicate that stringent control measures will be needed not just in China, but in several
other countries, to curtail the outbreak.

“This is unprecedented both in terms of the virus and the control measures that have been put in place,’
says Adam Kucharski, associate professor at the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine.
