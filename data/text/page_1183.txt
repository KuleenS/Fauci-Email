VL-01 reduced the cytokine release of IL-a/B, IL-6, MIP-1B, RANTES and TNF-a induced by LPS or
highly pathogen avian H5N1 influenza A virus. The present data demonstrates an antiviral effect of
VL-01 in vitro and in vivo and the ability to reduce influenza virus induced cytokines and
chemokines.
